JEE/JEE ADVANCE

Joint Entrance Examination (JEE) is an engineering entrance examination conducted for admission to pursue various undergraduate courses in top engineering institutes in India. It is constituted by two different examinations. - JEE Main and the JEE Advanced.JEE Main is a computer-based online test conducted by National Testing Agency. It is the first phase of the IIT Joint Entrance Exam.Joint Entrance Examination – Advanced is one of the toughest entrance exams in India conducted to screen students for admission to engineering courses in the prestigious Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs), Indian Institute of Science (IISc), and some other institutes. The exam is conducted in two stages – candidates need to clear JEE Main first in order to be eligible to appear for JEE Advanced. Around 12 to 14 lakh students appear for JEE Main, and only about 2.2 lakh students are selected to appear for JEE Advanced. Ultimately, approximately 11,000 students make it to the top engineering colleges in India. Reason for Being One of the Toughest Exams: Very few can crack the exam because of the high level of competition and extremely difficult paper.

BITSAT

BITSAT stands for Birla Institute of Technology and Science Admission Test. It is a national entrance test and if you qualify , you can get admission to one of the three BITS Pilani campuses situated in Hyderabad , Goa and Pilani.

VIT

VITEEE is conducted by the Vellore Institute of Technology every year for admission to undergraduate engineering courses (BTech) of participating institutes. Every year, VITEEE Exam is held to enrol students for various undergraduate courses. Around two Lakh students apply for VITEEE every year. All the eligible candidates will be offered admissions to B.Tech programmes at VIT Vellore, Chennai, Bhopal and Amravati.

SRMJEE

SRM Joint Engineering Entrance Examination or SRMJEEE is conducted by SRM Institute of Science and Technology (SRMIST) twice a year for admission to BTech programmes offered by the institute. SRMJEEE is a university-level exam that acts as a common entrance examination for all SRM Group institutes and universities located at Kattankulathur, Ramapuram, NCR – Ghaziabad, Ramapuram Part – Vadapalani, Amravati, Haryana, and Sikkim.

SET

Symbiosis Entrance Test (SET) is a general term used for the entrance exams conducted by the Symbiosis Institute (Deemed University) for admission into bachelors’ programs into their various branches.

NATA

The national aptitude test for architecture which is organised by the Council of Architecture. It is an aptitude test that an aspiring architecture student can give to get admission into architecture programmes in various colleges other than IITs and NITs. But bear in mind that unlike entrance tests for IITs and NITs, NATA is only an aptitude test and does not necessarily guarantee you admission into any college. The test consists of questions on general architecture history, geometry, shapes, materials, colour wheel etc., which in short are subjects that pertain to architecture.
