BJMC(Bachelor in Journalism and Mass Communication

BJMC (Bachelor of Journalism and Communication) is a three-duration undergraduate degree course for candidates who wish to build their career in the field of journalism and mass communication. After successfully completing the course candidates are able to develop as well as refine their skills of gathering, transmitting, and delivering information to a large audience.

Career opportunities for students in the field of mass communication are very high. Candidates who successfully complete BJMC can choose to work for news channels, newspapers, publishing houses, television, radio, internet, etc and earn a healthy remuneration

Eligiblity

The student should have a 12th passed degree from any recognised board. Student should have atleast of 50% to 60% in 12th boards.

Subjecvt you will be learning

  • Communicative Hindi
  • Still Photography
  • Application of Computer in Media
  • Print Journalism Lab
  • Media Management
  • Basics of Camera, Lights & Sound
  • Fundamentals of Economics & Indian Economy
  • ETC.

Entrance Exams

Most institutes or universities conduct entrance exams to offer admission to candidates in the BJMC course. Entrance exams of some institutions are also followed by a personal interview round

It may be noted that some institutes also provide direct admission to candidates on the basis of marks scored by them in Class 12th.

Jobs Opportuinites

  • Editor
  • Public Relation Officer
  • Radio Jockey
  • Video Jockey
  • Anchor
  • Reporter
  • Correspondent
  • ETC.
