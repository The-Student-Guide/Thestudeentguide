BA LLB(BAchelor of Arts - Legislative Law

BA LLB (Bachelor of Arts - Bachelor of Legislative Law) is a five-year integrated LLB course . T he Law course is an amalgamation of Humanities and Law streams, which allows candidates to study subjects such as History, Sociology, Administrative Law, Criminology, Family Law and so on.

Majority of the Law schools run semester system in which the five-year duration course is divided into 10 semesters.

Eligiblity

Candidates should clear Class 12 in any stream from a recognised school with minimum 45-50 per cent aggregated There is no age to pursue LAW.

Subject you will be learning

  • Law of Contract
  • Family Law
  • Property Law
  • Human Rights
  • Code of Civil Procedure
  • Professional Ethics
  • Taxation
  • Sociology
  • ETC.

Job Opportuinites

  • Litigation Lawyer
  • Judge
  • Legal Advisor
  • Company Secertary

Higher Studies

You can futher choose to take up either MBA or Master in LLM.
