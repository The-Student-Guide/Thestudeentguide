B.B.A(Bachelor in Business Management)

BBA Course is a three year undergraduate business management course that imparts managerial and entrepreneurial skills to students through BBA Subjects. BBA courses are available in various specializations such as Finance, Marketing and HR Management. Bachelor of Business Administration is availble to students from all streams like Arts, Science and Commerce.

The full form of BBA is Bachelor of Business Administration. As the BBA full form suggests, the BBA Syllabus is designed to impart adequate business administration skills to the students. BBA Subjects are Principles of Management, Macroeconomics, Human Behavior & Ethics at Workplace, Management Accounting, Banking & Insurance.
BBA course is very market and industry oriented. Students after completing the course get good BBA Jobs. The average BBA salary ranges between INR 2,50,000 to 10,00,000.

Eligiblity

The students should have passed 12th from a regonsied board . Students from any board can apply for BBA. The student should have a minimum of 50% in 12th standard.

Entrance Exam

IPU CET, NPAT.

Students may also get addmission on the basis of 12th board marks.
