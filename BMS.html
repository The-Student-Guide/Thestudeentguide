BMS(Bussiness Management Studies)

BMS (Bachelor of Management Studies) is a course that can be undertaken at an undergraduate level for a promising career in the field of management. This is a 3-year duration course that can be completed at a college or through distance learning. BMS is fast gaining popularity as many students are looking at other professional courses over the usual science, arts, and commerce.

Another reason for the BMS courses popularity is that it is a good course for starting a career. It is a professional course that has a lot of value in the professional world and BMS degree holders find good jobs right after college. The degree gives you an opportunity to pursue a career in the industry of your choice. These jobs are in management and administration, so the start pay is better than other bachelor degree holders. The BMS degree is definitely a good career option. Its is also known as the alternative of B.Com. In Post Graduation, it helps in taking up MBA as a option as it is more related to it than any other courses.

What you will be learning?

During the course of BMS you will be taught how to analyze and strategize the business realted situation. The students are also made to understand the importance of organizational hierarchy, teamwork, target-oriented attitude, leadership, problem-solving skills, and promptness in work in the BMS course.

Eligibilty

It will have a little advantage if you trake up Commerce stream in 12th. To take up BMS as a course you should have minimum 50% in 12th boards.
