Science

Students who choose Science as their stream in 11th and 12th Standard can also take up courses such as B.com and BMS(Means they can take up some of commerce and arts stream as well). To know more about courses in science

  • Engineering
  • Medical
  • Architecture
  • BCA(Bachelor in Computer Application)
  • B.Pharma
  • Nurse(B.Sc in Nursing)
  • CA(Chartered Accountant)

Commerce

Students who have completed their Junoir College from Commerce Stream can take up following courses in Degree College.

  • BMS (Bachelor of Management Studies)
  • BBA (Finance) (Bachelor in Business Administration with Specialization in Finance)
  • B.Com
  • BAF(Bachelor in Finance and Accountancy)
  • BJMC(Bachelor of Journalism and Mass Communication)
  • CA(Chartered Accountant)
Students of Commerce can take up courses from Science Stream while vice versa is possible. After Completing BBA you can take up MBA

Arts

Students who have completed their Junoir College from Arts Stream can take up following courses in Degree College.

  • BA (Bachelor of Arts)
  • BRS (Bachelor of Rural Studies)
  • BA LLB(LAW)
  • BFA (Bachelor in Financial Accounting)
  • BA in Psycology
To become a proper Psycologist you need to commplete your Ph.D(Doctorate) as well.
