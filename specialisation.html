Computer Science and Engineering

Computer science and information technology is the most demanding branch in engineering and the requirement in this field will continue to rise in the years to come. A career in computer science engineering is considered to be one of the most high-paying jobs and there are plenty of opportunities, IT companies employ a huge number of computer professionals every year. This is the branch of the most scope field in the engineering sector which is booming currently in India.Computer engineers can choose plenty of options to work in IT companies in various departments such as design, development, assembly, manufacture and maintenance, working as programmer, web developer, and e-commerce specialist, automotive companies, aerospace companies, etc. Job opportunities are available for graduates in government as well as the Private sector.

Mechanical Engineering

Mechanical Engineering is one of the oldest and broadest among engineering. It deals with manufacture and maintain machines and thus the students require an understanding in the core areas such as mechanics, dynamics, thermodynamics, materials science, structural analysis, and electricity. Mechanical Engineers can find employment in both private as well as government sectors. They are required to design, test, install, manufacture, operate and maintain an overview of machines and mechanical systems that are used in multiple industries.

Electrical & Electronics Engineering

Electrical engineers deal with designing electronic circuits and devices utilizing non-linear and active electrical components such as the diode, transistors and so on. It has a continuous demand for electricity boards, power sector organizations like NTPC, PSUs, railways, and defence forces. They are hired in the IT sector with various job opportunities. Electrical engineers will work on a wide range of components, devices, and systems, from small microchips to huge power station generators. Electrical Engineers design, test, develop and operate the manufacturing of electrical equipment, such as electric motors, navigation systems, communications systems and power generation equipment.

Civil Engineering

This branch involves the design, construction, and maintenance of work such as residences, institutional buildings, roads, bridges, canals, dams, airports, sewerage systems, pipelines, and railways. The subjects involved in civil engineering are materials science, geography, geology, soils, hydrology, environment, and mechanics. It has many sub-branches such as Coastal engineering, Construction engineering, Earthquake engineering, Environmental engineering, Forensic engineering, Geotechnical engineering, Materials science & engineering, Structural engineering, Transportation engineering, Municipal or urban engineering and so on.The task of a civil engineer is planning, designing, estimating, managing construction, execution, supervising construction, and maintenance of structures like building, roads, bridges, dams, etc.

Aeronautical Engineering

Aeronautical Engineering is one of the most interesting fields and one of the new engineering branches that consist of the development and construction of aircraft and spacecraft such as missiles, satellites, aeroplane and so on. The scope in aeronautical engineering is very reputed and challenging and also it is very good in order to have a paying job. Aeronautical Engineering is a specialized branch in the aviation industry.They are required in both public and private Airline Services, these engineers are available in Airlines, Air Force, Corporate Research Companies, Defence Ministry, Helicopter Companies, Aviation Companies, NASA and many others. Some of the top recruiters in this space are HAL, DRDO, ISRO, and others

Chemical Engineering

A chemical engineer is able to design large-scale processes to convert chemicals, raw materials into a number of useful products.They conduct research and develop processes of manufacturing chemicals. Top companies and organizations that hire chemical engineers are Reliance Industries, HPCL, IOCL, ONGC, NPCIL, CIL, etc. It is an ideal course for students who want to pursue a career in applied chemistry.

Biotechnology Engineering

Biotechnology is a unique branch of engineering the name itself projects that the word is a combination of biology and technology (Biotechnology = Biology + technology). This field is majorly used in the development of a few areas such as medical, agriculture, security and bio-research. this branch is a composition of engineering, chemistry, genetics, microbiology, and biochemistry.Biotechnology Engineering is a rapidly growing field. Biotechnology is also a research-oriented field.
