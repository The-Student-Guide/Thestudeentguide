BA in Psychology

BA Psychology is a 3-year full-time under-graduation program that includes the study of mind and behaviours. It consists of health, human development, personality, social behaviour, etc. The course is divided into 6 semesters with the first four semesters of theory subjects and practicals and the last semester dealing with internships and projects.

The Students will develop an understanding of Human nature. Psychologists are said to be highly satisfied with their careers as per research. Hence, opting for Psychology will be a great choice for career prospects

Eligiblity

The students should have completed thge 12th standard from any recognised boards. The students should also have at least of 50% in 12th boards.

Subject you will be studying

  • Statistical Methods for Psychological Research
  • Biopsychology
  • English Communication
  • Development of Psychological Though
  • Applied Social Psychology
  • Counselling Psychology
  • ETC.

Jobs Opportuinites

  • Career Counselors
  • Social Worker
  • Recruiter
  • ETC.

Higher Studies
