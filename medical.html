ALL ABOUT MEDICAL


As it s clear from the name , the medical stream is related to the medication line. If you are interested in medicines, formulations, diseases, diagnosis, wearing a white coat ,serve the human and humanity and medicinal research. Then go ahead for the medical stream. Since it is a growing field, you can find numerous exciting opportunities such as biomedical scientist, forensic scientist, clinical laboratory technologist, toxicologist, research scientist, healthcare scientist, pharmaceutical sales representative, consultant, patient care coordinator, etc. Medical is the second major stream with 37.70 lakh students enrolled at the undergraduate level.
