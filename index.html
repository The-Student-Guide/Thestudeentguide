Why do you need us?

Many students nowadays are drowning in the heaps of options of colleges and the career choices which can cause more stress in students life. That's where we come in. Our job is to facilitate you with various choices and option in order to make the process of decision making much more easier.

The Roadmap can be used by any students who has completed their studies till and after 10th. i.e who are in Junior College or in Degree College.





Features

Hostels

Many Students have come to gain better education here in Mumbai City. Students tend to loose all their time in the travelling and changing trains to reach the college. This does hurt their education at the end. So to prevent it many colleges have hostels and many places which are availabe for rent so students can either share the room or can live alone. Sharing helps the students to save their money and also helps to socialize a little more.

Internships

Some field need a requirement for internship in the course itself so to help the students we have also the getaway to the other certified websites which may help the students to get internships in a easier way. Students will help you reach to the right website which can help you get you your right Internships

Events

Very little a student can learn in four walls of a classroom and there is always a need to be on point with the latest updates and be more publicly relevant. So they tick all the boxes along with the curriculum and participating in various events is a way to go. Students will be provided with the link using which they can enroll for the events . This will surely provide you with enjoyment and recreation.
