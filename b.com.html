B.Com(Bachelors in Commerce)

B.Com full form is Bachelor of Commerce. BCom is a three year undergraduate program. B.Com course is designed to impart managerial skills to students while focusing on a particular business area.
During the 3-years B.Com degree, the aspirants will learn about the general business principles, and at the same time, study core commerce subjects such as finance, economics, accounting, actuarial studies, business management, etc. 0
The average fee for B.Com courses range from INR 5,000 – 50,000. After completing the degree students can expect job offers with minimum salary packages of INR 2,50,000 – 4,00,000 per annum.

Eligiblity

The minimum required for B.Com courses is 50% marks in class 12 or equivalent examination. The BCom course is the most popular and sought after course by the commerce students.

  • Accounts
  • Economics
